About the LinkedData.Center

LinkedData.Center is global service provider that wants to spread the use Linked Open Data in business

LinkedData.Center offers a fully managed, private instance of an RDF graph database, with a dedicated, password protected, SPARQL endpoint as a service.

LinkedData.Center targets developers and data-scientists who need to use linked (open) data and RDF graph database to develop their own applications.

For more information, visit http://LinkedData.Center/.
